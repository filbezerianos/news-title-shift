## Scoring Key

Each answer maps to a score of 1–4, where higher scores consistently indicate greater emotional reactivity, lower trust, stronger identity salience, higher threat perception, narrower media diet, more intuitive cognition, and worse current emotional state.

| Answer | Score |
|---|---|
| A | 1 |
| B | 2 |
| C | 3 |
| D | 4 |

This applies uniformly across all single-select questions, making every participant's profile **directly comparable and aggregatable**. For Q6 (ranking) and Q8/Q10 (multi-select), scoring is handled separately based on rank order and number of selections respectively.

**Total possible score: 16–64**, giving you a single composite reactivity and vulnerability index per participant to use as a variable in your research analysis.
